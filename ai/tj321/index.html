Aim Interactive introduces AI

Thursday, JUNE 3, 2021 Aim Interactive announces they are developing new games with almost everything developed by AI.

Aim Interactive's founder made a statement: "We are going to be using AI Technology for future games, such as making music, programming physics, and generating story. This will change the future of game development as we see it today.". Aim Interactives new AI will be public for partners in 2022 in a beta and soon after public release.

Aim Interactive Multiplayer games have never been the best, and when they were good, they were not online multiplayer, this AI Aim is developing will help the future of game development at AIM.
